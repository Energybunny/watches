Complications Complications
When talking about watches the term "complications" might come up, but what does it mean? A complication is basically another function that's been added to the watch and made the manufacturing process more "complicated".

Movements Movements
Quartz, mechanical, automatic, solar, mecha-quartz? What do these words even mean? They are different types of watch movements, eg different ways of making a watch "tick". The most common one in watches today is quartz, a battery powered movement. Quartz is most often seen in more affordable watches and when you look at more expensive models, they are usually mechanical. A mechanical movement is driven by a main spring that's been wound up either by hand or by the movement of your wrist during the day. Solar, automatic and mecha-quartz are different types of mechanical and quartz movements.
