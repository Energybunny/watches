About

Who am I? Why have I created this website? What will I do with it in the future? This secion will answer some of those questions and give you a general idea of my plan for this website.

Who am I?

My name is Daniel Wilsson (DW) and I come from Stockholm, Sweden. At the moment, I am located in the most southern part of sweden in a town called Trelleborg. I attend upper secodary school win Ystad. I have been doing webdesign since August 2018 and have been interested in watches since 2017. My current watch collection only consists of two watches; a Seiko sarb033 and a Citizen Eco-Drive AT2141-87E.

Why did I create this website?

This website started out as a school project and originally covered completely different subject. The idea was to make a website and fill it with content that I had alreadyu written before, in this case it was about PC components. I then felt that the site I had created was insuffcient for my own satisfaction so I decided to remake it. In order for me to have motivation, I made it about a subject I have a genuin interest in. Watches.

As the project continued I developed the website more and more. Eventually it reached a state where I felt I couldn't improve it more with my current skills. At this point I started filling it with content, such as text and images.
